Have Any Questions?

We're here to help you with any queries you might have about our services.
Feel free to browse through our FAQs.

  • Terms
  • What is Digistic ?
    Digistic is a platform designed for buying and selling digital assets with a focus on security, efficiency, and user-friendliness.
  • How does Digistic ensure security?
    We implement advanced encryption technologies and secure payment gateways to ensure your transactions are safe.
  • Can I track my transactions?
    Yes, you can easily track all your transactions through your account dashboard.
    • What types of assets can I sell?
    You can sell a variety of digital assets, including graphics, documents, videos, and software. Visit our "Upload" section for a complete list of supported formats.
  • What payment methods does Digistic accept?
    We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and bank transfers for your convenience.
  • Is there a minimum transaction amount?
    Yes, the minimum transaction amount is $10.
  • What is the payment process like?
    Once you select your assets, you can proceed to checkout and choose your preferred payment method to complete the transaction.
  • Are there any transaction fees?
    Yes, a small transaction fee is applied to cover processing costs.
  • How do refunds work?
    Refunds are processed based on eligibility and within our policy guidelines. Please review our Refund section for more details.
  • Can I get a receipt for my purchase?
    Yes, a receipt will be emailed to you after each transaction for your records.
  • How long does it take for the payment to process?
    Payments are usually processed within a few minutes, but may take longer during peak times.
  • How can I upload digital assets to Digistic?
    You can easily upload your digital assets by navigating to the upload section in your account and following the prompts.
  • What file formats are supported?
    We support a variety of file formats including JPG, PNG, GIF, PDF, and more.
  • Can I update my uploaded assets?
    Yes, you can edit or update your uploads through your account at any time.
  • Are there size limits for uploads?
    Yes, individual files cannot exceed 100 MB in size.
  • How long does it take for my uploads to be approved?
    Uploads are typically reviewed within 24 hours, after which you will be notified via email.
  • What is the refund policy?
    Our refund policy is designed to provide flexibility to our users. If you encounter any issues with your purchase, please review our refund criteria and process to determine eligibility.
  • How do I request a refund?
    To request a refund, contact our support team with the necessary transaction details. We'll review your case and respond within 2-3 business days.
  • Are there fees associated with refunds?
    Refunds may incur a small processing fee to cover transaction costs. This will be deducted from the refundable amount, if applicable.
  • How long does the refund process take?
    Refund processing typically takes 5-7 business days, depending on the payment method used. Once approved, you’ll receive a confirmation email.

    Note that bank processing times may vary.
  • What transactions are eligible for refunds?
    Eligible transactions include those that meet our refund criteria. Please consult our refund policy for full eligibility details.
  • What are the general terms of use for Digistic?
    By using Digistic, you agree to our terms and conditions, which govern the proper use of the platform, including transaction policies, user responsibilities, and data handling.
  • How does Digistic handle user data?
    We adhere to stringent data protection standards to safeguard your personal information. For more details, refer to our privacy policy.
  • Are there restrictions on digital assets?
    Yes, all assets uploaded or sold on Digistic must comply with our content guidelines. Any prohibited content will be removed, and the user may face penalties.
  • What are my rights as a Digistic user?
    As a user, you have the right to secure, transparent transactions and access to our services as long as you comply with our terms. Consult the terms section for more information.

    Digistic reserves the right to update these terms as necessary.
  • Are there penalties for policy violations?
    Yes, policy violations may result in account suspension, content removal, or further penalties depending on the severity of the breach.
